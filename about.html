My name is Aaron Albright, and I created this website because I love the Bible. I also happen to like web coding.

This small project is the result of combining both.

The Bible is an expansive source of all kinds of encouragment, regardless of your circumstance. The verses I've selected only scratch the surface of God's love letter to you. If you're curious at all about what the Bible says, I enourage you to read it for yourself.
